                            <div style="text-align: left;">
<p dir="ltr" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;"><strong id="docs-internal-guid-4a6e02ba-7fff-5750-4fc2-f18e0ae9357d"><img align="right" height="150" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/_uuH08qXol1wfmjS1gBg1fOr0fX3QcV-PK_g_uDP5WmdrUdZMKek5Z2reT-bqulSDbn6E2wn5dem9dmSbPwssdvObguOn57XE77f4kl-WclRLCNAvIDqYNVl6t61ujooqJHhFK-hoh5QuybDehfOcBw" style="border: 0px initial;width: 247px;height: 150px;margin: 0px;outline: none;text-decoration: none;-ms-interpolation-mode: bicubic;" width="247"></strong><span style="font-size:14px">The National Science Foundation (NSF) Computer and Information Science and Engineering (CISE) Directorate recently announced that applications are now being accepted for the third year of the <a href="https://www.nsf.gov/cise/CSGrad4US/" style="word-wrap: break-word;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #2731a1;font-weight: normal;text-decoration: underline;">CSGrad4US</a> Graduate Fellowship program. The 3-year fellowships support new Ph.D. students pursuing their degree in a CISE field which includes programs in Computer Science, Computer and Information Sciences, and Computer Engineering.&nbsp;</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;">This program has already changed the lives of 78 people, with 16 participants from Cohort 1 now attending CISE PhD programs and 62 participants from Cohort 2 receiving admissions offers from PhD programs this spring!</p>

<p dir="ltr" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong id="docs-internal-guid-4a6e02ba-7fff-5750-4fc2-f18e0ae9357d">Goal and Motivation&nbsp;</strong></span><br>
<br>
The goal is to increase the number of diverse, domestic graduate students pursuing research and innovation careers in the CISE fields. The fellowship program, which will provide 3-year fellowship opportunities for new Ph.D. students in the computing disciplines, was released in response to the increased demand for people with a Ph.D. in CISE fields, the continued decrease of domestic students pursuing research and completing a Ph.D., and the overall small number of bachelor’s degree recipients in CS pursuing graduate school. In particular, the percentage of domestic Ph.D. students in Computer Science graduating has decreased from 69% in 1985 to 31% in 2021 [1].<br>
<br>
NSF seeks candidates from a broad array of backgrounds and strongly encourages women, African Americans, Hispanics, American Indians, Alaska Natives, Native Hawaiians, Native Pacific Islanders, and persons with disabilities to apply.</p>

<p dir="ltr" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong id="docs-internal-guid-4a6e02ba-7fff-5750-4fc2-f18e0ae9357d">Benefits</strong></span></p>

<ul>
	<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr" style="-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;">
	<p dir="ltr" role="presentation" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;"><a href="https://cra.org/cra-wp/cs-grad4us/" target="_blank" style="word-wrap: break-word;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #2731a1;font-weight: normal;text-decoration: underline;">A year-long preparation program</a>, organized by CRA-E and CRA-WP, in which individuals selected for the Fellowship will receive mentoring support in identifying a graduate program, finding a research mentor, and applying to graduate programs; during this year, the individuals will also have opportunities to form a network with one another and with faculty advisors;<br>
	<br>
	For those who enroll in a qualifying doctoral degree-granting program at an institution of higher education having a campus located in the United States, its territories or possessions, or the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, an annual stipend of $37,000 for three years out of five; and<br>
	<br>
	Cost-of-education allowance of $16,000 per year for the three years noted above to the institution of higher education, which typically covers costs for all tuition, medical insurance, and institutional fees.</p>
	</li>
</ul>

<p dir="ltr" style="margin: 1em 0;padding: 0;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #00405a;font-family: Arial, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong id="docs-internal-guid-4a6e02ba-7fff-5750-4fc2-f18e0ae9357d">Timeline and Eligibility</strong><br>
<br>
To be eligible for the CSGrad4US Fellowship, &nbsp;applicants must have (1) graduated with a bachelor’s degree before December 31, 2022, (2) have demonstrated <a href="https://cra.org/csgrad4us/#Guidance" target="_blank" style="word-wrap: break-word;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #2731a1;font-weight: normal;text-decoration: underline;">CISE core competency</a>, (3) have not been enrolled in any degree-granting program since January 1, 2023, (4) have never enrolled in and have no pending application or offer of admission for a doctoral degree-granting program in a CISE discipline; and (5) will be prepared to attend graduate school by Fall 2025. &nbsp;Applications for the CSGrad4US Graduate Fellowship are due June 5, 2023.</span></p>
